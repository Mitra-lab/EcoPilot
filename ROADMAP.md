# Product Roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP Release
- **Carbon Assessment**: Calculate per-capita carbon scores.
- **AI Coach**: Local and Gemini-driven action items matching largest footprint sources.
- **Weekly Challenges**: Habit tracker and point incentives.
- **Self-Verification**: Completion logs with note auditing to prevent cheap-clicks.
- **Gamification**: Accumulating standings (Eco Starter to Planet Guardian) and 5 badge unlocks.
- **Resilient Local Experience**: Complete persistence via local storage matching database states.

---

## Phase 2: Cloud Persistence
- **Authentication**: User logins (Supabase Auth).
- **PostgreSQL Database Integration**: Sync local storage to cloud tables.
- **Row Level Security (RLS)**: Protect private household assessments.
- **Historical Analysis**: Track historical carbon score drops over months.

---

## Phase 3: Advanced AI Verification
- **Evidence Uploads**: Photo verification of actions (e.g. recycling bags, public transit tickets).
- **Utility Bill OCR**: Auto extraction of electricity and gas kilowatt-hours.
- **Gemini Vision Integration**: AI-driven visual auditing of evidence.

---

## Phase 4: Community Sustainability Platform
- **Community Challenges**: Collective neighborhood goals.
- **Leaderboards**: Friendly carbon reduction competition.
- **Sustainability Marketplace**: Partner discounts for high tier earners.
